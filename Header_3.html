CRATER LAKE

NATIONAL PARK

ABOUT

Crater Lake is a crater lake in south-central Oregon in the western United States.With a depth of 1,943 feet, Crater Lake is the deepest lake in the United States - and one of the most beautiful.Crater Lake rests in the belly of a dormant volcano. The volcano once stood 12,000 feet tall, but it collapsed after a major eruption 7,700 years ago.The eruptions formed Wizard Island, a cinder cone that rises from the water. The park has an abundance of fascinating volcanic features, including a second rocky island, the Phantom Ship.
